About Vistro

Dedicated to Global Growth Through Travel

Vistro Travel Consultancy Ltd is a tours and travel consultancy company based in Eldoret City. Incorporated on 21st June 2024, we are committed to providing high quality travel services with the help of a group of strong, enthusiastic and professional team. Each team member brings in a wealth of experience and knowledge of regional and international travel, tourism and hospitality to add to our communal pool of expertise. We also specialize in professional services such as travel consultancy, hotel reservations, flight bookings as well as course application.

  • Transparent and reliable travel services
  • Personalized education consulting
  • Global visa and immigration support
  • A strong network of international partners
